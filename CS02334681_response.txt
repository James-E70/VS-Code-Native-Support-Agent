Thank you for your eRequest and for the screenshot of shipment SBOM00003422.

The House Bill Type field on the shipment is currently blank and not editable. Based on the current product functionality, the House Bill Type field becomes read-only when the Consol attached to the shipment is set to type Direct (DRT). A Direct Consol is intended for shipments where the carrier issues the lowest Bill of Lading directly — it does not support issuing a House Bill of Lading.

To resolve this, please follow these steps:

1. Open the Consol linked to shipment SBOM00003422.
2. Check the Consol Type field. If it is set to Direct (DRT), change it to Agent (AGT).
3. Return to shipment SBOM00003422 > Basic Registration tab.
4. The House Bill Type field will now be editable. Set it to IAU - IT Club Australia (or your preferred HBL Type).
5. Since this is an FCL shipment, also go to the Packing tab and confirm all pack lines are linked to the relevant container.
6. Save the shipment and retry printing the HBL via Documents > Departure > Bill of Lading > Bill of Lading.

If, after changing the Consol type to AGT, the House Bill Type field remains blank or is still not editable, please provide a screenshot of the Consol's Basic Registration tab showing the Consol Type and we can investigate further.
